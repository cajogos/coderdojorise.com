Supporters

CoderDojo RISE is generously supported by awesome partners that provide pro-bono services or discounts on their own products to us and the wider CoderDojo community.

This contribution is provided by organisations and philanthropists that have similar visions and missions to CoderDojo. They're equally passionate about our vision: a world in which young people have the opportunity to learn code and be creative in a safe and social environment.
